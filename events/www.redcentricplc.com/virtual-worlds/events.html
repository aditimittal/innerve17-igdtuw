TECH MEME ATHON

Make a technical meme on a template of your choice.
♦ Meme should include at least ONE technical term or phrase
♦ Winners will be decided on the basis of direct likes. For a like to be counted, the page should be liked first.
♦ The top four memes will be awarded – two on the basis of number of likes, and two will be Jury’s choice.
♦ A maximum of two entries are allowed per person.
♦ PLAGIARISM IS NOT ALLOWED. Plagiarised content will be disqualified.
Date of Submission : 20--26 August, 2017
Participate and win exciting prizes!
Send in your entries, alongwith your name and college to: innerve.techevents@gmail.com
